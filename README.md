# Corporate Climber - Business RPG

A web-based turn-based business RPG where you climb the corporate ladder from fresh graduate to successful professional.

## How to Play

1. **Open the Game**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Create Your Character**: Enter your name and select your college degree
3. **Start Your Career**: Begin with $1,000 but face weekly expenses of $700
4. **Find Employment**: Browse and apply for jobs based on your qualifications
5. **Advance Time**: Click "Advance 1 Week" to progress and earn money
6. **Manage Finances**: Monitor your balance sheet and net worth

## Features

### MVP Complete ✅
- **Character Creation**: Name selection and college degree choice
- **Job System**: Multiple job types with different salary levels and requirements
- **Turn-Based Gameplay**: Advance 1 week at a time
- **Financial Management**: Complete balance sheet with income, expenses, and net worth
- **Job Application**: Apply for positions with success rates based on qualifications
- **Weekly Updates**: Detailed financial reports each week

### Game Mechanics

**Starting Conditions:**
- Initial cash: $1,000
- Weekly expenses: $700 ($500 living + $200 student loans)
- Goal: Find employment to avoid going broke

**College Degrees & Job Matching:**
- **Business Administration**: Sales, Financial Analyst, Marketing, Project Coordination
- **Computer Science**: Junior Developer, Data Analyst  
- **Engineering**: Junior Developer, Data Analyst, Project Coordination
- **Finance**: Financial Analyst, Sales
- **Marketing**: Marketing Coordinator, Sales
- **Economics**: Financial Analyst, Data Analyst, Sales
- **Liberal Arts**: Customer Service, Marketing

**Job Salary Range:**
- Entry Level: $400-450/week
- Mid Level: $600-750/week  
- Higher Level: $800-900/week
